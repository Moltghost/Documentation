# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026


## 1. Introduction

MoltGhost ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use our services, including the MoltGhost platform, App Manager, Agent Pods, and any associated APIs, dashboards, or documentation (collectively, the "Services"). By using the Services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.


## 2. Our Privacy Architecture

MoltGhost is designed as a privacy-first platform. Agent Pods are isolated environments where all user data (including conversations, memory, wallet keys, model weights, and agent configuration) is stored and processed locally. MoltGhost does not have access to the contents of your Agent Pod. The platform can manage the pod's lifecycle (start, stop, backup) but cannot read, analyze, or extract data from inside the pod. This architectural separation is fundamental to how the platform operates, not an optional feature.


## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide

Account registration information (email address, authentication credentials). Agent deployment configuration (GPU selection, model selection, pod settings). Support inquiries and communications. Feedback and survey responses.

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers). Usage data (features accessed, interactions, session duration). Network information (IP address, browser type, referring URLs). Platform metrics (pod status, resource utilization, tunnel connectivity).

### 3.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, access, or store: (a) conversation history or chat logs between you and your agents; (b) agent memory or long-term knowledge data; (c) wallet private keys, seed phrases, or wallet credentials; (d) Private Skill execution data or blockchain transaction details; (e) files, datasets, or any data stored inside Agent Pods; (f) model inference inputs or outputs.


## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes: (a) providing, maintaining, and improving the Services; (b) provisioning and managing Agent Pods and associated infrastructure; (c) processing account-level billing and payments; (d) responding to support requests and communications; (e) monitoring platform performance, security, and stability; (f) detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and technical issues; (g) complying with applicable legal obligations; (h) generating aggregated, anonymized analytics to improve the Services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.


## 5. Agent Pod Data

All data inside an Agent Pod is owned and controlled exclusively by the user. This includes conversation history, Private Memory data, wallet keys, model configuration, Private Skill execution results, and any files stored on the pod's local disk. MoltGhost processes this data only within the pod's isolated environment and does not transmit, copy, or access it outside the pod boundary. When a pod is terminated, all data inside it is permanently destroyed. When Private Backup is used, data is encrypted inside the pod before being transmitted to Storj decentralized storage. MoltGhost does not hold the encryption keys and cannot decrypt backup data.


## 6. Blockchain Data

Transactions conducted on the Solana blockchain through standard methods are publicly visible. MoltGhost's Private Skills are designed to shield transaction details (addresses, amounts, links) from public observers on-chain. However, MoltGhost does not control the Solana blockchain and cannot guarantee that privacy mechanisms will be effective under all circumstances. We do not collect or store private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords at any point.


## 7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with: (a) infrastructure providers who assist in operating the Services (RunPod for compute, Cloudflare for networking, Storj for backup storage), subject to their respective privacy policies; (b) law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by applicable law, legal process, or government request; (c) other parties in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to affected users. We do not share personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. We cannot share Agent Pod data because we do not have access to it.


## 8. Data Retention

We retain account-level information for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Agent Pod data is retained only within the pod and is permanently destroyed upon pod termination. Encrypted backups on Storj are retained according to the user's configuration. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us through official channels.


## 9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest, access controls, pod-level isolation, and regular security assessments. Agent Pod isolation ensures that each agent's data is separated at the hardware level from all other agents. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information: (a) access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you; (b) correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information; (c) deletion: request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements; (d) portability: request a machine-readable copy of your information; (e) objection: object to certain processing activities; (f) restriction: request restriction of processing in certain circumstances. To exercise these rights, contact us through official channels. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.


## 11. International Transfers

MoltGhost operates globally. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. Agent Pods may be provisioned in various geographic regions depending on GPU availability. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.


## 12. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.


## 13. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our web-based Services may use cookies and similar tracking technologies for authentication, preferences, and analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Services.


## 14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Services or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance.
