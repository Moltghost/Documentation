## Token Utility

MOLTG is the native token of the MoltGhost ecosystem. All platform services are paid in SOL or USDC, while MOLTG serves as the core identity, access, and governance layer of the platform.

- **Tier Access.** Holding MOLTG unlocks premium platform features, priority GPU provisioning, and early access to new capabilities as they are released.
- **Governance.** MOLTG holders participate in platform governance, influencing decisions on feature priorities, protocol parameters, and ecosystem direction.
- **Fee Discount.** Users who hold MOLTG receive reduced service fees when using the platform. The more you hold, the better your rate.
- **Community Identity.** MOLTG represents membership in the MoltGhost ecosystem. Holders are part of the community that shapes the future of private AI agents.
- **Development Fund.** Platform fees collected from service usage are allocated toward ongoing development, infrastructure improvements, and new feature builds.

Additional utility mechanisms will be introduced as the platform evolves through each phase of the roadmap.
